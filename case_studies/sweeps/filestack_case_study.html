About Sweeps

Sweeps is a labor marketplace for moving, odd jobs, and more. The platform connects local workers with homeowners and businesses, serving over 5,000 workers and 10,000+ customers.

In a marketplace built on trust, visual verification matters. All members have profile photos, onboarding videos, and can create and upload files throughout their workflow.
