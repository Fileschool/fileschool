About Ready Set Recover

Ready Set Recover empowers people undergoing surgery, and their loved-ones, to prepare more intelligently and recover more confidently. The platform serves patients, surgical care partners, and "Surgery Supporters" with a digital platform that blends guided content, community, and interactivity.

At its core, the platform facilitates interactions that go beyond text. Users embed photos and videos, chat dynamically, and share encouragement and updates. One of the signature features: Surgery Supporters can submit short surprise-videos of support just before surgery, which are delivered through the platform for inspiration.

How they measure success: User upload completion rate, engagement with embedded media, reduction in tech-support tickets, and qualitative feedback from patients and supporters.

"Filestack is the seamless upload and media-delivery platform that let us reliably bring supporter photos and videos into our surgical-journey app with zero friction."

Integration

The development team integrated Filestack's File Picker API into the "Surgery Supporter" section of Ready Set Recover. They configured the picker to accept photo and video files, and tied uploads into their chat-module so supporters could embed media inline with messages.

They also built a specific flow for their "surprise video" moment: supporters receive a prompt in the app, record their clip, upload via Filestack, and the system delivers that video to the patient ahead of surgery. On the backend, the file link is stored via Filestack, with minimal downstream processing required.
